Project Highlights

BUSINESS ENVIRONMENT

Turbhe, strategically located near prominent business hubs like MIDC, Dhirubhai Ambani Knowledge City, and Millennium Business Park, is an ideal choice for a range of industries.

SKILLED TALENT POOL

With numerous educational institutions in close proximity, Turbhe provides businesses with access to a large, well-educated, and skilled workforce.

COST-EFFECTIVE RENTAL

Turbhe offers competitive commercial rental rates compared to other areas in Mumbai, making it a cost-effective option for businesses.

FUTURE GROWTH AND ROI

The upcoming Mumbai Trans Harbour Link will significantly reduce travel time to South Mumbai, increasing land value and ensuring high returns on investment.

FIVE-STAR HOTELS

The development of multiple five-star hotels in the vicinity adds convenience and luxury for business travelers, further enhancing Turbhe's appeal as a prime office location.
